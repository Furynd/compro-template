About Us

PT Inspectindo Mediatama Drilling and Blasting Services (IMT Drilling and Blasting) is a division PT Inspectindo Mediatama whose a private national company which was established in Jakarta in year 2005. IMT Drilling and Blasting concern on providing any rock on ground solutions for the construction, mining, reclamation and quarry industries. IMT Drilling and Blasting comprises a group of dedicated professionals with hands on experience and thorough knowledge of drilling and blasting. IMT Drilling and Blasting boasts an excellent safety record, we strive to offer our clients custom solutions to make them more competitive. IMT Drilling and Blasting is a turn key company with knowledge and experience, products and services.
